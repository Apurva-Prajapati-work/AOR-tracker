How It Works

Four steps to your real timeline.

No account needed. Your data is anonymised and contributes back to the community automatically.

01
Enter your AOR date
Input your Acknowledgement of Receipt date and immigration stream. 30 seconds — no account required.
Free · No account
02
See your cohort
Instantly compare your position against everyone who applied in the same month and stream.
Real data · Not IRCC averages
03
Log your milestones
Update BIL, BGC, medicals, and PPR as they happen. Each update improves accuracy for your cohort.
Contributes to community
04
Get community alerts
WES delays, IRCC tracker bugs, and cohort anomalies — reported by real applicants on Discord.

Open Source Community

Your feedback shapes the roadmap.

Submit bugs, feature requests, or data corrections from the tracker. Each submission auto-creates a GitHub issue — no GitHub account needed.

💬
Submit without a GitHub account
Fill a simple form on the tracker. We handle the GitHub issue creation automatically with proper labels and routing.
👍
Upvote to prioritise
Community upvotes determine what we build next. Most-wanted features rise to the top of the public roadmap.
🛠️
Self-assign and contribute code
Developers can claim open issues via GitHub OAuth and contribute — no repo write access required.
